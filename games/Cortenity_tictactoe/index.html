Your Path to Victory

How to Play

Master the grid with four simple principles. Every mark brings you closer to neon glory.

01

Pick Your Mode

Choose to battle the AI with three difficulty levels or challenge a friend in local pass-and-play mode.

02

Place Your Mark

Click any empty cell to place your glowing ✕ or ○. Each mark pulses with neon energy as it locks into the grid.

03

Think Ahead

Anticipate your opponent's moves. Block their lines while building your own path to three-in-a-row.

04

Win the Round

Line up three marks horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Watch the neon victory line illuminate your triumph.
